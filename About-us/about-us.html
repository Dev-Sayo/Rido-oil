ABOUT US

Welcome to Rido Palm Oil, your trusted partner in delivering the finest palm oil straight from the heart of Nigeria. Founded in 2020 amid the vibrant landscapes of Nigeria's palm-rich regions, we set out with a simple yet powerful vision: to revolutionize the way palm oil reaches tables across the nation and beyond. From humble beginnings as a family-driven initiative, we've grown into a reliable supplier committed to excellence, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.

OUR STORY

Born in the lush oil palm belt of Nigeria, Rido Palm Oil was established by a team of passionate entrepreneurs who recognized the untapped potential of locally sourced, high-quality palm oil. In a market flooded with inconsistent products, we vowed to stand out by prioritizing purity, freshness, and ethical sourcing. Today, we're proud to be a cornerstone of Nigeria's palm oil industry, blending traditional farming wisdom with modern processing techniques to ensure every drop meets the highest standards.

COMMITMENT TO QUALITY

At Rido, quality isn't just a promise—it's our foundation. We source our palm oil exclusively from certified, sustainable farms, employing rigorous quality controls from harvest to bottling. Our oil is rich in nutrients, free from additives, and packed with the natural golden hue and nutty aroma that makes Nigerian palm oil legendary. Whether it's for frying, baking, or everyday cooking, you can trust Rido for palm oil that's as versatile as it is superior.
